Welcome to Firefly Guesthouse - The Berlin Angkor. Firefly is a German and Spanish owned and managed guesthouse located very close to the entrance of the Angkor Wat Conservation Area. The guesthouse is situated in a safe neighborhood, very quite at night, where you can still experience the real Cambodian life and just a 5 minute tuktuk ride from the hassle and bustle of the center of town (Old Market and Pub St.). Stay in a nice and cozy guesthouse with all the comforts of a hotel, on the budget of a guesthouse. Relax or enjoy the sunset at our rooftop bar and restaurant .
