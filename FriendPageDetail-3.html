Angkor Spirit Palace Resort & Spa
www.angkorspiritpalace.com

The wonderful building occupied by the Angkor Spirit Palace was built in 1988 as a cultural centre to teach aspiring artists the gentle but exacting art of traditional Apsara dancing.

The building is unique and displays many elements of traditional Khmer architecture. The owner/operators of this new hotel - Mr Khim Chamroeun and Mrs Khim Chandany - saw the potential to use this wonderful building as a hotel and to share it with visitors to Siem Reap.
