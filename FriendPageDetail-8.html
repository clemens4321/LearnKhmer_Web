Milo Music Cambodia
www.milo-music.org/

Milo (ミロ) is a Japanese band in Siem Reap, Cambodia formed in 2007 by the pianist Aya Urata. It’s more than just a band, it’s an organization for music education support and planning music events in Cambodia.

Mission:
  • Training for Cambodian musicians
  • Music education support
  • Planning of music event
  • Rebirth of Khmer traditional music
