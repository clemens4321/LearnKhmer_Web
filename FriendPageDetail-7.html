KHMERBIRD - Cambodian Blogger
www.khmerbird.com

I started KhmerBird in 2008, initially to share information about Cambodia. At that time there was a lack of updated information about the country. I want to provide a real-time update via my blog to the world that Cambodia is not a scary country as it have been reported in the medias. Time passed and we are now in the a good way of rebuilding the country.

My main goal for KhmerBird is to make it become a place where Cambodian local people can provide useful information such as travel guides, good places to visit or best things to do in the country.
